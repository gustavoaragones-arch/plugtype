Compatibility summary

Your Uruguay plug may fit in Nepal outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Uruguay plug types: C F L

Nepal plug types: C D M

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F L and C D M), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Uruguay: 230V, 50Hz

Nepal: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
