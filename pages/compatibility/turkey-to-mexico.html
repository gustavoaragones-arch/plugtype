Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mexico; plug types used there differ from Turkey. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Turkey plug types: C F

Mexico plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Turkey (C F) and Mexico (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Turkey: 230V, 50Hz

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Turkey 230V vs Mexico 127V).
