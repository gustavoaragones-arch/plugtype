Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mongolia; plug types used there differ from Japan. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Japan plug types: A B

Mongolia plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Japan (A B) and Mongolia (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Japan: 100V, 50Hz

Mongolia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Japan 100V vs Mongolia 230V).
