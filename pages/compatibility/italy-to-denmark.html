Plug comparison

Italy plug types: C F L

Denmark plug types: C K

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F L and C K), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Denmark: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
