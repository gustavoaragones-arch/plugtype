Compatibility summary

Your British Virgin Islands plug may fit in Cambodia outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

British Virgin Islands plug types: A B

Cambodia plug types: A B C F G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B C F G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Cambodia: 230V, 50Hz
