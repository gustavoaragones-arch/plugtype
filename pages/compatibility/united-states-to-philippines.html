Can You Use a United States Plug in Philippines?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your United States plug may fit in Philippines outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

United States plug types: A B

Philippines plug types: A B C

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B C), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

United States: 120V, 60Hz

Philippines: 220V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (United States 120V vs Philippines 220V).
