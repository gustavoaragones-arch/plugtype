Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in New Caledonia; plug types used there differ from Singapore.

Plug comparison

Singapore plug types: G

New Caledonia plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Singapore (G) and New Caledonia (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Singapore: 230V, 50Hz

New Caledonia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
