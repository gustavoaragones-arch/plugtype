Can You Use a Philippines Plug in Jersey?

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Jersey; plug types used there differ from Philippines.

Plug comparison

Philippines plug types: A B C

Jersey plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Philippines (A B C) and Jersey (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Philippines: 220V, 60Hz

Jersey: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
