Compatibility summary

Your Morocco plug may fit in South Africa outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Morocco plug types: C E

South Africa plug types: C D M N

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C D M N), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Morocco: 230V, 50Hz

South Africa: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
