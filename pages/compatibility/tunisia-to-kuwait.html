Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Kuwait; plug types used there differ from Tunisia.

Plug comparison

Tunisia plug types: C E

Kuwait plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Tunisia (C E) and Kuwait (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Tunisia: 230V, 50Hz

Kuwait: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
