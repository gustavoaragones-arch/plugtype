Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Christmas Island; plug types used there differ from Belarus.

Plug comparison

Belarus plug types: C F

Christmas Island plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Belarus (C F) and Christmas Island (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Belarus: 230V, 50Hz

Christmas Island: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
