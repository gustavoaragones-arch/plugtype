Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Vanuatu; plug types used there differ from Ukraine.

Plug comparison

Ukraine plug types: C F

Vanuatu plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Ukraine (C F) and Vanuatu (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Ukraine: 230V, 50Hz

Vanuatu: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
