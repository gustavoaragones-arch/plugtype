Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in United Kingdom; plug types used there differ from Micronesia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Micronesia plug types: A B

United Kingdom plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Micronesia (A B) and United Kingdom (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Micronesia: 120V, 60Hz

United Kingdom: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Micronesia 120V vs United Kingdom 230V).
