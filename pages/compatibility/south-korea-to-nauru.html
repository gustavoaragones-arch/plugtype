Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Nauru; plug types used there differ from South Korea.

Plug comparison

South Korea plug types: C F

Nauru plug types: I

There is no common plug type between South Korea (C F) and Nauru (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Korea: 220V, 60Hz

Nauru: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
