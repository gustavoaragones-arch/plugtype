Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Gibraltar; plug types used there differ from Saint Barthélemy.

Plug comparison

Saint Barthélemy plug types: C E

Gibraltar plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Saint Barthélemy (C E) and Gibraltar (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Barthélemy: 230V, 60Hz

Gibraltar: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
