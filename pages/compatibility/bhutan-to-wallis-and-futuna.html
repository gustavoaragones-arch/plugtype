Compatibility summary

Your Bhutan plug may fit in Wallis and Futuna outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Bhutan plug types: C D M

Wallis and Futuna plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C D M and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bhutan: 230V, 50Hz

Wallis and Futuna: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
