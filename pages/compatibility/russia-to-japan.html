Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Japan; plug types used there differ from Russia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Russia plug types: C F

Japan plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Russia (C F) and Japan (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Russia: 230V, 50Hz

Japan: 100V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Russia 230V vs Japan 100V).
