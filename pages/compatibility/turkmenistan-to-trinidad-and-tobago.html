Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Trinidad and Tobago; plug types used there differ from Turkmenistan. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Turkmenistan plug types: C F

Trinidad and Tobago plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Turkmenistan (C F) and Trinidad and Tobago (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Turkmenistan: 230V, 50Hz

Trinidad and Tobago: 115V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Turkmenistan 230V vs Trinidad and Tobago 115V).
