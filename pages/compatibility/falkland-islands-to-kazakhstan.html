Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Kazakhstan; plug types used there differ from Falkland Islands.

Plug comparison

Falkland Islands plug types: G

Kazakhstan plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Falkland Islands (G) and Kazakhstan (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Falkland Islands: 240V, 50Hz

Kazakhstan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
