Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Uganda; plug types used there differ from Mayotte.

Plug comparison

Mayotte plug types: C E

Uganda plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Mayotte (C E) and Uganda (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mayotte: 230V, 50Hz

Uganda: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
