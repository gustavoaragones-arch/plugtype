Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Micronesia; plug types used there differ from Hong Kong. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Hong Kong plug types: G

Micronesia plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Hong Kong (G) and Micronesia (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Hong Kong: 220V, 50Hz

Micronesia: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Hong Kong 220V vs Micronesia 120V).
