Compatibility summary

Your Saint Pierre and Miquelon plug may fit in Hungary outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon plug types: C E

Hungary plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon: 230V, 50Hz

Hungary: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
