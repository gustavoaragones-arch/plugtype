Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in French Guiana; plug types used there differ from Jersey.

Plug comparison

Jersey plug types: G

French Guiana plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Jersey (G) and French Guiana (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Jersey: 230V, 50Hz

French Guiana: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
