Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Ghana; plug types used there differ from Guyana.

Plug comparison

Guyana plug types: A B

Ghana plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Guyana (A B) and Ghana (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Guyana: 220V, 50Hz

Ghana: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
