Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in South Africa; plug types used there differ from Guernsey.

Plug comparison

Guernsey plug types: G

South Africa plug types: C D M N

There is no common plug type between Guernsey (G) and South Africa (C D M N). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Guernsey: 230V, 50Hz

South Africa: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
