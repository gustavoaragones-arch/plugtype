Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Vanuatu; plug types used there differ from Gambia.

Plug comparison

Gambia plug types: G

Vanuatu plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Gambia (G) and Vanuatu (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Gambia: 230V, 50Hz

Vanuatu: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
