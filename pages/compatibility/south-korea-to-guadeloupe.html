Compatibility summary

Your South Korea plug may fit in Guadeloupe outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

South Korea plug types: C F

Guadeloupe plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

South Korea: 220V, 60Hz

Guadeloupe: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
