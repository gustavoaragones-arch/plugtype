Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sudan; plug types used there differ from Taiwan. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Taiwan plug types: A B

Sudan plug types: C D G

There is no common plug type between Taiwan (A B) and Sudan (C D G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Taiwan: 110V, 60Hz

Sudan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Taiwan 110V vs Sudan 230V).
