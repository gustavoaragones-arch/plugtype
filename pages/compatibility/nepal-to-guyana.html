Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guyana; plug types used there differ from Nepal.

Plug comparison

Nepal plug types: C D M

Guyana plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Nepal (C D M) and Guyana (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Nepal: 230V, 50Hz

Guyana: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
