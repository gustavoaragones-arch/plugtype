Compatibility summary

Your Italy plug may fit in Belarus outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Italy plug types: C F L

Belarus plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F L and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Italy: 230V, 50Hz

Belarus: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
