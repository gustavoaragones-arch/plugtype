Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Jersey; plug types used there differ from Morocco.

Plug comparison

Morocco plug types: C E

Jersey plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Morocco (C E) and Jersey (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Morocco: 230V, 50Hz

Jersey: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
