Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Northern Cyprus; plug types used there differ from Panama. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Panama plug types: A B

Northern Cyprus plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Panama (A B) and Northern Cyprus (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Panama: 120V, 60Hz

Northern Cyprus: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Panama 120V vs Northern Cyprus 230V).
