Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Solomon Islands; plug types used there differ from Croatia.

Plug comparison

Croatia plug types: C F

Solomon Islands plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Croatia (C F) and Solomon Islands (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Croatia: 230V, 50Hz

Solomon Islands: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
