Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Dominica; plug types used there differ from Palestine.

Plug comparison

Palestine plug types: C H

Dominica plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Palestine (C H) and Dominica (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Palestine: 230V, 50Hz

Dominica: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
