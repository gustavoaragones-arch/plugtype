Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cayman Islands; plug types used there differ from South Sudan. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

South Sudan plug types: C D G

Cayman Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between South Sudan (C D G) and Cayman Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Sudan: 230V, 50Hz

Cayman Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (South Sudan 230V vs Cayman Islands 120V).
