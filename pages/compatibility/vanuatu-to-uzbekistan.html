Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Uzbekistan; plug types used there differ from Vanuatu.

Plug comparison

Vanuatu plug types: I

Uzbekistan plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Vanuatu (I) and Uzbekistan (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Vanuatu: 230V, 50Hz

Uzbekistan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
