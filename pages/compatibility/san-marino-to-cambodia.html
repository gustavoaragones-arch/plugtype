Compatibility summary

Your San Marino plug may fit in Cambodia outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

San Marino plug types: C F L

Cambodia plug types: A B C F G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F L and A B C F G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

San Marino: 230V, 50Hz

Cambodia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
