Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Greece; plug types used there differ from Costa Rica. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Costa Rica plug types: A B

Greece plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Costa Rica (A B) and Greece (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Greece: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Costa Rica 120V vs Greece 230V).
