Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Kosovo; plug types used there differ from United States. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

United States plug types: A B

Kosovo plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between United States (A B) and Kosovo (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

United States: 120V, 60Hz

Kosovo: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (United States 120V vs Kosovo 230V).
