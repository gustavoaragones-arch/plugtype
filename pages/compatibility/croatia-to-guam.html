Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guam; plug types used there differ from Croatia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Croatia plug types: C F

Guam plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Croatia (C F) and Guam (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Croatia: 230V, 50Hz

Guam: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Croatia 230V vs Guam 110V).
