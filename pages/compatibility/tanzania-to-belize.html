Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Belize; plug types used there differ from Tanzania. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Tanzania plug types: G

Belize plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Tanzania (G) and Belize (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Tanzania: 230V, 50Hz

Belize: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Tanzania 230V vs Belize 110V).
