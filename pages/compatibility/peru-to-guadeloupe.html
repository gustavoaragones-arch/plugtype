Compatibility summary

Your Peru plug may fit in Guadeloupe outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Peru plug types: A B C

Guadeloupe plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Peru: 230V, 60Hz

Guadeloupe: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
