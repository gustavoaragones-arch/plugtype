Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Papua New Guinea; plug types used there differ from Macau.

Plug comparison

Macau plug types: G

Papua New Guinea plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Macau (G) and Papua New Guinea (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Macau: 230V, 50Hz

Papua New Guinea: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
