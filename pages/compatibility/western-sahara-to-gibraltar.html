Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Gibraltar; plug types used there differ from Western Sahara.

Plug comparison

Western Sahara plug types: C E

Gibraltar plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Western Sahara (C E) and Gibraltar (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Western Sahara: 230V, 50Hz

Gibraltar: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
