Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Vatican City; plug types used there differ from Oman.

Plug comparison

Oman plug types: G

Vatican City plug types: C F L

There is no common plug type between Oman (G) and Vatican City (C F L). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Oman: 230V, 50Hz

Vatican City: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
