Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Barthélemy; plug types used there differ from Canada. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Canada plug types: A B

Saint Barthélemy plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Canada (A B) and Saint Barthélemy (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Canada: 120V, 60Hz

Saint Barthélemy: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Canada 120V vs Saint Barthélemy 230V).
