Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cook Islands; plug types used there differ from Curaçao. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Curaçao plug types: A B

Cook Islands plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Curaçao (A B) and Cook Islands (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Curaçao: 127V, 50Hz

Cook Islands: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Curaçao 127V vs Cook Islands 240V).
