Compatibility summary

Your Switzerland plug may fit in Poland outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Switzerland plug types: C J

Poland plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C J and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Switzerland: 230V, 50Hz

Poland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
