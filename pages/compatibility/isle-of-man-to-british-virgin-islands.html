Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in British Virgin Islands; plug types used there differ from Isle of Man. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Isle of Man plug types: G

British Virgin Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Isle of Man (G) and British Virgin Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Isle of Man: 230V, 50Hz

British Virgin Islands: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Isle of Man 230V vs British Virgin Islands 110V).
