Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Ghana; plug types used there differ from North Macedonia.

Plug comparison

North Macedonia plug types: C F

Ghana plug types: G

There is no common plug type between North Macedonia (C F) and Ghana (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

North Macedonia: 230V, 50Hz

Ghana: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
