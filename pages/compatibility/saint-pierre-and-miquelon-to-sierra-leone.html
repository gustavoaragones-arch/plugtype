Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sierra Leone; plug types used there differ from Saint Pierre and Miquelon.

Plug comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon plug types: C E

Sierra Leone plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Saint Pierre and Miquelon (C E) and Sierra Leone (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon: 230V, 50Hz

Sierra Leone: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
