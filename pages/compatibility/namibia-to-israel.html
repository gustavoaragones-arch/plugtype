Compatibility summary

Your Namibia plug may fit in Israel outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Namibia plug types: C M N

Israel plug types: C H

Both countries share at least one plug type (C M N and C H), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Namibia: 230V, 50Hz

Israel: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
