Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Thailand; plug types used there differ from Norfolk Island.

Plug comparison

Norfolk Island plug types: I

Thailand plug types: A B C O

There is no common plug type between Norfolk Island (I) and Thailand (A B C O). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Norfolk Island: 230V, 50Hz

Thailand: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
