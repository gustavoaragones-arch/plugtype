Compatibility summary

Your Dominican Republic plug may fit in China outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Dominican Republic plug types: A B

China plug types: A C I

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A C I), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Dominican Republic: 120V, 60Hz

China: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Dominican Republic 120V vs China 220V).
