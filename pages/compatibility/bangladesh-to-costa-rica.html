Compatibility summary

Your Bangladesh plug may fit in Costa Rica outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Bangladesh plug types: A C D M G

Costa Rica plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A C D M G and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bangladesh: 230V, 50Hz

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Bangladesh 230V vs Costa Rica 120V).
