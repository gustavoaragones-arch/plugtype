Compatibility summary

Your Netherlands plug may fit in Chile outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Netherlands plug types: C F

Chile plug types: C L

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C L), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Netherlands: 230V, 50Hz

Chile: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
