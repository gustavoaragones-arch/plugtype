Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Somaliland; plug types used there differ from Puerto Rico. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Puerto Rico plug types: A B

Somaliland plug types: C G

There is no common plug type between Puerto Rico (A B) and Somaliland (C G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Puerto Rico: 120V, 60Hz

Somaliland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Puerto Rico 120V vs Somaliland 230V).
