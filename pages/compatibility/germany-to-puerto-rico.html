Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Puerto Rico; plug types used there differ from Germany. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Germany plug types: C F

Puerto Rico plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Germany (C F) and Puerto Rico (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Germany: 230V, 50Hz

Puerto Rico: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Germany 230V vs Puerto Rico 120V).
