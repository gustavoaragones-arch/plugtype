Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in British Virgin Islands; plug types used there differ from Cocos (Keeling) Islands. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Cocos (Keeling) Islands plug types: I

British Virgin Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Cocos (Keeling) Islands (I) and British Virgin Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Cocos (Keeling) Islands: 230V, 50Hz

British Virgin Islands: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Cocos (Keeling) Islands 230V vs British Virgin Islands 110V).
