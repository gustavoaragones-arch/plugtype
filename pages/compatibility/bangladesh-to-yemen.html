Compatibility summary

Your Bangladesh plug may fit in Yemen outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Bangladesh plug types: A C D M G

Yemen plug types: C D G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A C D M G and C D G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bangladesh: 230V, 50Hz

Yemen: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
