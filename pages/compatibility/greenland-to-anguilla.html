Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Anguilla; plug types used there differ from Greenland. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Greenland plug types: C F K

Anguilla plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Greenland (C F K) and Anguilla (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Greenland: 230V, 50Hz

Anguilla: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Greenland 230V vs Anguilla 110V).
