Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Seychelles; plug types used there differ from Saint Martin.

Plug comparison

Saint Martin plug types: C E

Seychelles plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Saint Martin (C E) and Seychelles (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Martin: 230V, 60Hz

Seychelles: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
