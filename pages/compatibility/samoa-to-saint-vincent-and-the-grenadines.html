Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines; plug types used there differ from Samoa.

Plug comparison

Samoa plug types: I

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

There is no common plug type between Samoa (I) and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines (A B G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Samoa: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
