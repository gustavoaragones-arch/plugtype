Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Democratic Republic of the Congo; plug types used there differ from Palau. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Palau plug types: A B

Democratic Republic of the Congo plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Palau (A B) and Democratic Republic of the Congo (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Palau: 120V, 60Hz

Democratic Republic of the Congo: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Palau 120V vs Democratic Republic of the Congo 230V).
