Compatibility summary

Your Isle of Man plug may fit in Saint Kitts and Nevis outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Isle of Man plug types: G

Saint Kitts and Nevis plug types: A B G

Both countries share at least one plug type (G and A B G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Isle of Man: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Kitts and Nevis: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
