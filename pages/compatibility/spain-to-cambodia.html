Compatibility summary

Your Spain plug may fit in Cambodia outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Spain plug types: C F

Cambodia plug types: A B C F G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and A B C F G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Spain: 230V, 50Hz

Cambodia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
