Can You Use a United States Plug in Turks and Caicos Islands?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your United States plug may fit in Turks and Caicos Islands outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

United States plug types: A B

Turks and Caicos Islands plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

United States: 120V, 60Hz

Turks and Caicos Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
