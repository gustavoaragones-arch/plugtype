Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mauritius; plug types used there differ from Canada. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Canada plug types: A B

Mauritius plug types: C E G

There is no common plug type between Canada (A B) and Mauritius (C E G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Canada: 120V, 60Hz

Mauritius: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Canada 120V vs Mauritius 230V).
