Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mayotte; plug types used there differ from U.S. Virgin Islands. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

U.S. Virgin Islands plug types: A B

Mayotte plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between U.S. Virgin Islands (A B) and Mayotte (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

U.S. Virgin Islands: 110V, 60Hz

Mayotte: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (U.S. Virgin Islands 110V vs Mayotte 230V).
