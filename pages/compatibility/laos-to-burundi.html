Compatibility summary

Your Laos plug may fit in Burundi outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Laos plug types: A B C

Burundi plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Laos: 230V, 50Hz

Burundi: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
