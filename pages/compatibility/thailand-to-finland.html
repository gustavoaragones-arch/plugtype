Can You Use a Thailand Plug in Finland?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your Thailand plug may fit in Finland outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Thailand plug types: A B C O

Finland plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C O and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Thailand: 220V, 50Hz

Finland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
