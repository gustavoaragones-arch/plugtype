Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Wallis and Futuna; plug types used there differ from Cuba. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Cuba plug types: A B

Wallis and Futuna plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Cuba (A B) and Wallis and Futuna (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Cuba: 120V, 60Hz

Wallis and Futuna: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Cuba 120V vs Wallis and Futuna 230V).
