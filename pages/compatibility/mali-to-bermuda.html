Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bermuda; plug types used there differ from Mali. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mali plug types: C E

Bermuda plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Mali (C E) and Bermuda (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mali: 230V, 50Hz

Bermuda: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mali 230V vs Bermuda 120V).
