Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Pakistan; plug types used there differ from Macau.

Plug comparison

Macau plug types: G

Pakistan plug types: C D

There is no common plug type between Macau (G) and Pakistan (C D). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Macau: 230V, 50Hz

Pakistan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
