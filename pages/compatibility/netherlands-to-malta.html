Can You Use a Netherlands Plug in Malta?

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Malta; plug types used there differ from Netherlands.

Plug comparison

Netherlands plug types: C F

Malta plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Netherlands (C F) and Malta (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Netherlands: 230V, 50Hz

Malta: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
