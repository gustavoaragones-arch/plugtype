Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Wallis and Futuna; plug types used there differ from Isle of Man.

Plug comparison

Isle of Man plug types: G

Wallis and Futuna plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Isle of Man (G) and Wallis and Futuna (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Isle of Man: 230V, 50Hz

Wallis and Futuna: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
