Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Australia; plug types used there differ from Iceland.

Plug comparison

Iceland plug types: C F

Australia plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Iceland (C F) and Australia (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Iceland: 230V, 50Hz

Australia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
