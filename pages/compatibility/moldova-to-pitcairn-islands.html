Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Pitcairn Islands; plug types used there differ from Moldova.

Plug comparison

Moldova plug types: C F

Pitcairn Islands plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Moldova (C F) and Pitcairn Islands (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Moldova: 230V, 50Hz

Pitcairn Islands: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
