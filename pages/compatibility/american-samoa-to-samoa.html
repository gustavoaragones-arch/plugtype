Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Samoa; plug types used there differ from American Samoa. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

American Samoa plug types: A B

Samoa plug types: I

There is no common plug type between American Samoa (A B) and Samoa (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

American Samoa: 120V, 60Hz

Samoa: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (American Samoa 120V vs Samoa 230V).
