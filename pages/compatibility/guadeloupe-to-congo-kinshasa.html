Your Guadeloupe plug may fit in Democratic Republic of the Congo outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Democratic Republic of the Congo plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
