Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saudi Arabia; plug types used there differ from Western Sahara.

Plug comparison

Western Sahara plug types: C E

Saudi Arabia plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Western Sahara (C E) and Saudi Arabia (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Western Sahara: 230V, 50Hz

Saudi Arabia: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
