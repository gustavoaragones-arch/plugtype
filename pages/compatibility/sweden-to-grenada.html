Can You Use a Sweden Plug in Grenada?

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Grenada; plug types used there differ from Sweden.

Plug comparison

Sweden plug types: C F

Grenada plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Sweden (C F) and Grenada (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Sweden: 230V, 50Hz

Grenada: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
