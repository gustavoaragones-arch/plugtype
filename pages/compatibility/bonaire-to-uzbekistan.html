Compatibility summary

Your Bonaire plug may fit in Uzbekistan outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Bonaire plug types: A B C F

Uzbekistan plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C F and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bonaire: 127V, 50Hz

Uzbekistan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Bonaire 127V vs Uzbekistan 230V).
