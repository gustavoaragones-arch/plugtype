Can You Use a Brazil Plug in Anguilla?

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Anguilla; plug types used there differ from Brazil.

Plug comparison

Brazil plug types: C N

Anguilla plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Brazil (C N) and Anguilla (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Brazil: 127V, 60Hz

Anguilla: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
