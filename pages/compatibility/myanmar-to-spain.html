Compatibility summary

Your Myanmar plug may fit in Spain outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Myanmar plug types: A B C D E F G I

Spain plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C D E F G I and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Myanmar: 230V, 50Hz

Spain: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
