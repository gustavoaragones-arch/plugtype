Compatibility summary

Your Niger plug may fit in Chad outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Niger plug types: C E

Chad plug types: C E F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C E F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Niger: 230V, 50Hz

Chad: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
