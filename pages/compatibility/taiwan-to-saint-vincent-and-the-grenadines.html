Compatibility summary

Your Taiwan plug may fit in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Taiwan plug types: A B

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Taiwan: 110V, 60Hz

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Taiwan 110V vs Saint Vincent and the Grenadines 230V).
