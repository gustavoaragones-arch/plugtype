Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in French Polynesia; plug types used there differ from Guernsey.

Plug comparison

Guernsey plug types: G

French Polynesia plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Guernsey (G) and French Polynesia (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Guernsey: 230V, 50Hz

French Polynesia: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
