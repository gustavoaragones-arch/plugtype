Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Belize; plug types used there differ from Switzerland. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Switzerland plug types: C J

Belize plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Switzerland (C J) and Belize (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Switzerland: 230V, 50Hz

Belize: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Switzerland 230V vs Belize 110V).
