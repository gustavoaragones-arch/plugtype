Compatibility summary

Your Brazil plug may fit in France outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Brazil plug types: C N

France plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C N and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Brazil: 127V, 60Hz

France: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Brazil 127V vs France 230V).
