Can You Use a Netherlands Plug in Papua New Guinea?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Papua New Guinea; plug types used there differ from Netherlands.

Plug comparison

Netherlands plug types: C F

Papua New Guinea plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Netherlands (C F) and Papua New Guinea (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Netherlands: 230V, 50Hz

Papua New Guinea: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
