Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in El Salvador; plug types used there differ from Somalia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Somalia plug types: C G

El Salvador plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Somalia (C G) and El Salvador (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Somalia: 230V, 50Hz

El Salvador: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Somalia 230V vs El Salvador 120V).
