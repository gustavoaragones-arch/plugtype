Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Colombia; plug types used there differ from South Africa. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

South Africa plug types: C D M N

Colombia plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between South Africa (C D M N) and Colombia (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Africa: 230V, 50Hz

Colombia: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (South Africa 230V vs Colombia 110V).
