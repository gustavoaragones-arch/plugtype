Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in El Salvador; plug types used there differ from French Guiana. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

French Guiana plug types: C E

El Salvador plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between French Guiana (C E) and El Salvador (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

French Guiana: 230V, 50Hz

El Salvador: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (French Guiana 230V vs El Salvador 120V).
