Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saudi Arabia; plug types used there differ from Timor-Leste.

Plug comparison

Timor-Leste plug types: C E F I

Saudi Arabia plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Timor-Leste (C E F I) and Saudi Arabia (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Timor-Leste: 230V, 50Hz

Saudi Arabia: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
