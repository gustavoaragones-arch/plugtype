Compatibility summary

Your Monaco plug may fit in Bonaire outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Monaco plug types: C E

Bonaire plug types: A B C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and A B C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Monaco: 230V, 50Hz

Bonaire: 127V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Monaco 230V vs Bonaire 127V).
