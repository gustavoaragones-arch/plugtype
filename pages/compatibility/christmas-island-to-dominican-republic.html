Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Dominican Republic; plug types used there differ from Christmas Island. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Christmas Island plug types: I

Dominican Republic plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Christmas Island (I) and Dominican Republic (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Christmas Island: 230V, 50Hz

Dominican Republic: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Christmas Island 230V vs Dominican Republic 120V).
