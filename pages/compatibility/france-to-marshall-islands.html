Can You Use a France Plug in Marshall Islands?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Marshall Islands; plug types used there differ from France. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

France plug types: C E

Marshall Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between France (C E) and Marshall Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

France: 230V, 50Hz

Marshall Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (France 230V vs Marshall Islands 120V).
