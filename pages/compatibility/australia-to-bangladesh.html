Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bangladesh; plug types used there differ from Australia.

Plug comparison

Australia plug types: I

Bangladesh plug types: A C D M G

There is no common plug type between Australia (I) and Bangladesh (A C D M G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Australia: 230V, 50Hz

Bangladesh: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
