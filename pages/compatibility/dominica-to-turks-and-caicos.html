Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Turks and Caicos Islands; plug types used there differ from Dominica. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Dominica plug types: G

Turks and Caicos Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Dominica (G) and Turks and Caicos Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Dominica: 230V, 50Hz

Turks and Caicos Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Dominica 230V vs Turks and Caicos Islands 120V).
