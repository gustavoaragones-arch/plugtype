Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Gambia; plug types used there differ from French Polynesia.

Plug comparison

French Polynesia plug types: C E

Gambia plug types: G

There is no common plug type between French Polynesia (C E) and Gambia (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

French Polynesia: 230V, 60Hz

Gambia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
