Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Senegal; plug types used there differ from Saint Lucia.

Plug comparison

Saint Lucia plug types: G

Senegal plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Saint Lucia (G) and Senegal (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Lucia: 230V, 50Hz

Senegal: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
