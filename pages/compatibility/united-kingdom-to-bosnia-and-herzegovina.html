Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bosnia and Herzegovina; plug types used there differ from United Kingdom.

Plug comparison

United Kingdom plug types: G

Bosnia and Herzegovina plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between United Kingdom (G) and Bosnia and Herzegovina (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

United Kingdom: 230V, 50Hz

Bosnia and Herzegovina: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
