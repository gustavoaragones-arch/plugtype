Compatibility summary

Your Bosnia and Herzegovina plug may fit in Italy outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Bosnia and Herzegovina plug types: C F

Italy plug types: C F L

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F L), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bosnia and Herzegovina: 230V, 50Hz

Italy: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
