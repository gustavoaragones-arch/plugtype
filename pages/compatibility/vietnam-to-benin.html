Compatibility summary

Your Vietnam plug may fit in Benin outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Vietnam plug types: A C F

Benin plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (A C F and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Vietnam: 220V, 50Hz

Benin: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
