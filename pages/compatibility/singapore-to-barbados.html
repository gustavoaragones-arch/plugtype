Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Barbados; plug types used there differ from Singapore. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Singapore plug types: G

Barbados plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Singapore (G) and Barbados (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Singapore: 230V, 50Hz

Barbados: 115V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Singapore 230V vs Barbados 115V).
