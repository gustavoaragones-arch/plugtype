Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in American Samoa; plug types used there differ from Montenegro. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Montenegro plug types: C F

American Samoa plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Montenegro (C F) and American Samoa (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Montenegro: 230V, 50Hz

American Samoa: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Montenegro 230V vs American Samoa 120V).
