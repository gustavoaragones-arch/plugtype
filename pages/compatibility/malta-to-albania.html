Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Albania; plug types used there differ from Malta.

Plug comparison

Malta plug types: G

Albania plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Malta (G) and Albania (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Malta: 230V, 50Hz

Albania: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
