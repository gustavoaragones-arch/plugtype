Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Greenland; plug types used there differ from Kiribati.

Plug comparison

Kiribati plug types: I

Greenland plug types: C F K

There is no common plug type between Kiribati (I) and Greenland (C F K). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Kiribati: 240V, 50Hz

Greenland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
