Compatibility summary

Your Central African Republic plug may fit in Moldova outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Central African Republic plug types: C E

Moldova plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Central African Republic: 230V, 50Hz

Moldova: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
