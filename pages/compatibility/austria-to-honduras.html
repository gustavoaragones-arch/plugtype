Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Honduras; plug types used there differ from Austria. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Austria plug types: C F

Honduras plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Austria (C F) and Honduras (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Austria: 230V, 50Hz

Honduras: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Austria 230V vs Honduras 120V).
