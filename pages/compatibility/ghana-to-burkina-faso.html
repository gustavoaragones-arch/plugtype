Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Burkina Faso; plug types used there differ from Ghana.

Plug comparison

Ghana plug types: G

Burkina Faso plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Ghana (G) and Burkina Faso (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Ghana: 230V, 50Hz

Burkina Faso: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
