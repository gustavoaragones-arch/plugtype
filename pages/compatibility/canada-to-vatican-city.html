Can You Use a Canada Plug in Vatican City?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Vatican City; plug types used there differ from Canada. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Canada plug types: A B

Vatican City plug types: C F L

There is no common plug type between Canada (A B) and Vatican City (C F L). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Canada: 120V, 60Hz

Vatican City: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Canada 120V vs Vatican City 230V).
