Compatibility summary

Your Barbados plug may fit in Saint Kitts and Nevis outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Barbados plug types: A B

Saint Kitts and Nevis plug types: A B G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Barbados: 115V, 50Hz

Saint Kitts and Nevis: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Barbados 115V vs Saint Kitts and Nevis 230V).
