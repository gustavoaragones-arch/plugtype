Compatibility summary

Your Nicaragua plug may fit in Vietnam outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Nicaragua plug types: A B

Vietnam plug types: A C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Nicaragua: 120V, 60Hz

Vietnam: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Nicaragua 120V vs Vietnam 220V).
