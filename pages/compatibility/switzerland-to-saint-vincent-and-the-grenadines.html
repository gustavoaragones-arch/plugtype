Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines; plug types used there differ from Switzerland.

Plug comparison

Switzerland plug types: C J

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

There is no common plug type between Switzerland (C J) and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines (A B G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Switzerland: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
