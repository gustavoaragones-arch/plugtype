Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Greece; plug types used there differ from Suriname. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Suriname plug types: A B

Greece plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Suriname (A B) and Greece (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Suriname: 110V, 60Hz

Greece: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Suriname 110V vs Greece 230V).
