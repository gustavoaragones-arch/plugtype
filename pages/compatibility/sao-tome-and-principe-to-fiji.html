Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Fiji; plug types used there differ from São Tomé and Príncipe.

Plug comparison

São Tomé and Príncipe plug types: C F

Fiji plug types: I

There is no common plug type between São Tomé and Príncipe (C F) and Fiji (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

São Tomé and Príncipe: 230V, 50Hz

Fiji: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
