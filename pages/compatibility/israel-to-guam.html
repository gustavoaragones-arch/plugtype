Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guam; plug types used there differ from Israel. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Israel plug types: C H

Guam plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Israel (C H) and Guam (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Israel: 230V, 50Hz

Guam: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Israel 230V vs Guam 110V).
