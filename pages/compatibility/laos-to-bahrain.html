Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bahrain; plug types used there differ from Laos.

Plug comparison

Laos plug types: A B C

Bahrain plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Laos (A B C) and Bahrain (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Laos: 230V, 50Hz

Bahrain: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
