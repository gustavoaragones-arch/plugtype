Compatibility summary

Your Sierra Leone plug may fit in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Sierra Leone plug types: G

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

Both countries share at least one plug type (G and A B G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Sierra Leone: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
