Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Barbados; plug types used there differ from Sudan. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Sudan plug types: C D G

Barbados plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Sudan (C D G) and Barbados (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Sudan: 230V, 50Hz

Barbados: 115V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Sudan 230V vs Barbados 115V).
