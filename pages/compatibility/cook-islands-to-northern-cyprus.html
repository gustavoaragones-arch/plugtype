Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Northern Cyprus; plug types used there differ from Cook Islands.

Plug comparison

Cook Islands plug types: I

Northern Cyprus plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Cook Islands (I) and Northern Cyprus (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Cook Islands: 240V, 50Hz

Northern Cyprus: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
