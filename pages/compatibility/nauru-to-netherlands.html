Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Netherlands; plug types used there differ from Nauru.

Plug comparison

Nauru plug types: I

Netherlands plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Nauru (I) and Netherlands (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Nauru: 240V, 50Hz

Netherlands: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
