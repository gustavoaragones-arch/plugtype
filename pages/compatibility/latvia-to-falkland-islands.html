Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Falkland Islands; plug types used there differ from Latvia.

Plug comparison

Latvia plug types: C F

Falkland Islands plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Latvia (C F) and Falkland Islands (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Latvia: 230V, 50Hz

Falkland Islands: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
