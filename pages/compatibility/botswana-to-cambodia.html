Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cambodia; plug types used there differ from Botswana.

Plug comparison

Botswana plug types: M

Cambodia plug types: A B C F G

There is no common plug type between Botswana (M) and Cambodia (A B C F G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Botswana: 230V, 50Hz

Cambodia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
