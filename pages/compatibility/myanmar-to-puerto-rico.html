Compatibility summary

Your Myanmar plug may fit in Puerto Rico outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Myanmar plug types: A B C D E F G I

Puerto Rico plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C D E F G I and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Myanmar: 230V, 50Hz

Puerto Rico: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Myanmar 230V vs Puerto Rico 120V).
