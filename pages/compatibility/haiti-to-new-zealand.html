Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in New Zealand; plug types used there differ from Haiti. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Haiti plug types: A B

New Zealand plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Haiti (A B) and New Zealand (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Haiti: 110V, 60Hz

New Zealand: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Haiti 110V vs New Zealand 230V).
