Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in China; plug types used there differ from Grenada.

Plug comparison

Grenada plug types: G

China plug types: A C I

There is no common plug type between Grenada (G) and China (A C I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Grenada: 230V, 50Hz

China: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
