Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Malta; plug types used there differ from Canada. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Canada plug types: A B

Malta plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Canada (A B) and Malta (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Canada: 120V, 60Hz

Malta: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Canada 120V vs Malta 230V).
