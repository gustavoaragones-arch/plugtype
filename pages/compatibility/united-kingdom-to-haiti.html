Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Haiti; plug types used there differ from United Kingdom. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

United Kingdom plug types: G

Haiti plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between United Kingdom (G) and Haiti (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

United Kingdom: 230V, 50Hz

Haiti: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (United Kingdom 230V vs Haiti 110V).
