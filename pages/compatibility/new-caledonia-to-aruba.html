Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Aruba; plug types used there differ from New Caledonia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

New Caledonia plug types: C E

Aruba plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between New Caledonia (C E) and Aruba (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

New Caledonia: 230V, 50Hz

Aruba: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (New Caledonia 230V vs Aruba 120V).
