Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Zimbabwe; plug types used there differ from New Zealand.

Plug comparison

New Zealand plug types: I

Zimbabwe plug types: G

There is no common plug type between New Zealand (I) and Zimbabwe (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

New Zealand: 230V, 50Hz

Zimbabwe: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
