Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Tanzania; plug types used there differ from Monaco.

Plug comparison

Monaco plug types: C E

Tanzania plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Monaco (C E) and Tanzania (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Monaco: 230V, 50Hz

Tanzania: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
