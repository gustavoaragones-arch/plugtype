Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Ireland; plug types used there differ from French Guiana.

Plug comparison

French Guiana plug types: C E

Ireland plug types: G

There is no common plug type between French Guiana (C E) and Ireland (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

French Guiana: 230V, 50Hz

Ireland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
