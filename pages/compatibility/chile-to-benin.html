Compatibility summary

Your Chile plug may fit in Benin outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Chile plug types: C L

Benin plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C L and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Chile: 220V, 50Hz

Benin: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
