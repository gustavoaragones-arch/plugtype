Can You Use a Singapore Plug in Mauritius?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your Singapore plug may fit in Mauritius outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Singapore plug types: G

Mauritius plug types: C E G

Both countries share at least one plug type (G and C E G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Singapore: 230V, 50Hz

Mauritius: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
