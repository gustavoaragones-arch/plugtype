Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Brunei; plug types used there differ from Montenegro.

Plug comparison

Montenegro plug types: C F

Brunei plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Montenegro (C F) and Brunei (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Montenegro: 230V, 50Hz

Brunei: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
