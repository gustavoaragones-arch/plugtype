Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Dominica; plug types used there differ from Aruba. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Aruba plug types: A B

Dominica plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Aruba (A B) and Dominica (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Aruba: 120V, 60Hz

Dominica: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Aruba 120V vs Dominica 230V).
