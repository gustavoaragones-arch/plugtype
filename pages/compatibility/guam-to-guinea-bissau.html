Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guinea-Bissau; plug types used there differ from Guam. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Guam plug types: A B

Guinea-Bissau plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Guam (A B) and Guinea-Bissau (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Guam: 110V, 60Hz

Guinea-Bissau: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Guam 110V vs Guinea-Bissau 230V).
