Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Kiribati; plug types used there differ from Albania.

Plug comparison

Albania plug types: C F

Kiribati plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Albania (C F) and Kiribati (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Albania: 230V, 50Hz

Kiribati: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
