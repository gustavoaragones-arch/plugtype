Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in United States; plug types used there differ from Ethiopia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Ethiopia plug types: C F G

United States plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Ethiopia (C F G) and United States (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Ethiopia: 230V, 50Hz

United States: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Ethiopia 230V vs United States 120V).
