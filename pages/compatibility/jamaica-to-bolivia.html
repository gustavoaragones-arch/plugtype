Compatibility summary

Your Jamaica plug may fit in Bolivia outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Jamaica plug types: A B

Bolivia plug types: A B C

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B C), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Jamaica: 110V, 50Hz

Bolivia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Jamaica 110V vs Bolivia 230V).
