Compatibility summary

Your Angola plug may fit in Kazakhstan outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Angola plug types: C F

Kazakhstan plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Kazakhstan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
