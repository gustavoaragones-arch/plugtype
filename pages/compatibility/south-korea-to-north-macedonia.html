Plug comparison

South Korea plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

North Macedonia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
