Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Turkey; plug types used there differ from Tanzania.

Plug comparison

Tanzania plug types: G

Turkey plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Tanzania (G) and Turkey (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Tanzania: 230V, 50Hz

Turkey: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
