Compatibility summary

Your Djibouti plug may fit in Laos outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Djibouti plug types: C E

Laos plug types: A B C

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and A B C), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Djibouti: 230V, 50Hz

Laos: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
