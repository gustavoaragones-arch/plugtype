Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Costa Rica; plug types used there differ from Mayotte. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mayotte plug types: C E

Costa Rica plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Mayotte (C E) and Costa Rica (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mayotte: 230V, 50Hz

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mayotte 230V vs Costa Rica 120V).
