Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Ecuador; plug types used there differ from Monaco. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Monaco plug types: C E

Ecuador plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Monaco (C E) and Ecuador (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Monaco: 230V, 50Hz

Ecuador: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Monaco 230V vs Ecuador 120V).
