Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Panama; plug types used there differ from Kiribati. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Kiribati plug types: I

Panama plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Kiribati (I) and Panama (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Kiribati: 240V, 50Hz

Panama: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Kiribati 240V vs Panama 120V).
