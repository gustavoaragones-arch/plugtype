Compatibility summary

Your Turks and Caicos Islands plug may fit in Mexico outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Turks and Caicos Islands plug types: A B

Mexico plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Turks and Caicos Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
