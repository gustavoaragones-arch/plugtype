Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Palau; plug types used there differ from Burkina Faso. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Burkina Faso plug types: C E

Palau plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Burkina Faso (C E) and Palau (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Burkina Faso: 230V, 50Hz

Palau: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Burkina Faso 230V vs Palau 120V).
