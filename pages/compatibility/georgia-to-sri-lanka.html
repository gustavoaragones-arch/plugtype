Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sri Lanka; plug types used there differ from Georgia.

Plug comparison

Georgia plug types: C F

Sri Lanka plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Georgia (C F) and Sri Lanka (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Georgia: 230V, 50Hz

Sri Lanka: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
