Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Lebanon; plug types used there differ from Tokelau.

Plug comparison

Tokelau plug types: I

Lebanon plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Tokelau (I) and Lebanon (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Tokelau: 230V, 50Hz

Lebanon: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
