Compatibility summary

Your Trinidad and Tobago plug may fit in Curaçao outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Trinidad and Tobago plug types: A B

Curaçao plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Trinidad and Tobago: 115V, 60Hz

Curaçao: 127V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
