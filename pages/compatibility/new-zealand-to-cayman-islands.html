Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cayman Islands; plug types used there differ from New Zealand. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

New Zealand plug types: I

Cayman Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between New Zealand (I) and Cayman Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

New Zealand: 230V, 50Hz

Cayman Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (New Zealand 230V vs Cayman Islands 120V).
