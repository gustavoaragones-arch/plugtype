Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Syria; plug types used there differ from Kenya.

Plug comparison

Kenya plug types: G

Syria plug types: C E F L

There is no common plug type between Kenya (G) and Syria (C E F L). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Kenya: 240V, 50Hz

Syria: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
