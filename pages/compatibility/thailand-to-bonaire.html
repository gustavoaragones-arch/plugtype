Can You Use a Thailand Plug in Bonaire?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your Thailand plug may fit in Bonaire outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Thailand plug types: A B C O

Bonaire plug types: A B C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C O and A B C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Thailand: 220V, 50Hz

Bonaire: 127V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Thailand 220V vs Bonaire 127V).
