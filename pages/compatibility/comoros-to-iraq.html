Compatibility summary

Your Comoros plug may fit in Iraq outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Comoros plug types: C E

Iraq plug types: C G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E and C G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Comoros: 230V, 50Hz

Iraq: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
