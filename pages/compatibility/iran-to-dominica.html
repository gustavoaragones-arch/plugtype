Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Dominica; plug types used there differ from Iran.

Plug comparison

Iran plug types: C F

Dominica plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Iran (C F) and Dominica (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Iran: 230V, 50Hz

Dominica: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
