Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Algeria; plug types used there differ from Saint Vincent and the Grenadines.

Plug comparison

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

Algeria plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Saint Vincent and the Grenadines (A B G) and Algeria (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Algeria: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
