Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bosnia and Herzegovina; plug types used there differ from Belize. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Belize plug types: A B

Bosnia and Herzegovina plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Belize (A B) and Bosnia and Herzegovina (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Belize: 110V, 60Hz

Bosnia and Herzegovina: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Belize 110V vs Bosnia and Herzegovina 230V).
