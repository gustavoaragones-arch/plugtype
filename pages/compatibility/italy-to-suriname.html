Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Suriname; plug types used there differ from Italy. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Italy plug types: C F L

Suriname plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Italy (C F L) and Suriname (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Italy: 230V, 50Hz

Suriname: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Italy 230V vs Suriname 110V).
