Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guatemala; plug types used there differ from Saint Martin. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Saint Martin plug types: C E

Guatemala plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Saint Martin (C E) and Guatemala (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Martin: 230V, 60Hz

Guatemala: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Saint Martin 230V vs Guatemala 120V).
