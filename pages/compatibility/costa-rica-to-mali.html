Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mali; plug types used there differ from Costa Rica. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Costa Rica plug types: A B

Mali plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Costa Rica (A B) and Mali (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Mali: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Costa Rica 120V vs Mali 230V).
