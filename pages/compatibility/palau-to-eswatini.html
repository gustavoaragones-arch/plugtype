Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Eswatini; plug types used there differ from Palau. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Palau plug types: A B

Eswatini plug types: C M N

There is no common plug type between Palau (A B) and Eswatini (C M N). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Palau: 120V, 60Hz

Eswatini: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Palau 120V vs Eswatini 230V).
