Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Samoa; plug types used there differ from Jamaica. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Jamaica plug types: A B

Samoa plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Jamaica (A B) and Samoa (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Jamaica: 110V, 50Hz

Samoa: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Jamaica 110V vs Samoa 230V).
