Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Brunei; plug types used there differ from Mexico. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mexico plug types: A B

Brunei plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Mexico (A B) and Brunei (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Brunei: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mexico 127V vs Brunei 240V).
