Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Equatorial Guinea; plug types used there differ from Marshall Islands. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Marshall Islands plug types: A B

Equatorial Guinea plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Marshall Islands (A B) and Equatorial Guinea (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Marshall Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Equatorial Guinea: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Marshall Islands 120V vs Equatorial Guinea 230V).
