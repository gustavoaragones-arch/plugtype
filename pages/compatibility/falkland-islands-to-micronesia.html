Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Micronesia; plug types used there differ from Falkland Islands. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Falkland Islands plug types: G

Micronesia plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Falkland Islands (G) and Micronesia (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Falkland Islands: 240V, 50Hz

Micronesia: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Falkland Islands 240V vs Micronesia 120V).
