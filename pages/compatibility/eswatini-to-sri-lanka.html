Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sri Lanka; plug types used there differ from Eswatini.

Plug comparison

Eswatini plug types: C M N

Sri Lanka plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Eswatini (C M N) and Sri Lanka (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Eswatini: 230V, 50Hz

Sri Lanka: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
