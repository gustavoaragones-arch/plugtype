Compatibility summary

Your Antigua and Barbuda plug may fit in Venezuela outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Antigua and Barbuda plug types: A B

Venezuela plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.
