Compatibility summary

Your Cambodia plug may fit in Romania outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Cambodia plug types: A B C F G

Romania plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B C F G and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Cambodia: 230V, 50Hz

Romania: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
