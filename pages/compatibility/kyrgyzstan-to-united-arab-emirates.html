Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in United Arab Emirates; plug types used there differ from Kyrgyzstan.

Plug comparison

Kyrgyzstan plug types: C F

United Arab Emirates plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Kyrgyzstan (C F) and United Arab Emirates (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Kyrgyzstan: 230V, 50Hz

United Arab Emirates: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
