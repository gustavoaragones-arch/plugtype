Compatibility summary

Your Germany plug may fit in Mozambique outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Germany plug types: C F

Mozambique plug types: C F M N

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F M N), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Germany: 230V, 50Hz

Mozambique: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
