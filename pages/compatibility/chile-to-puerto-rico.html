Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Puerto Rico; plug types used there differ from Chile. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Chile plug types: C L

Puerto Rico plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Chile (C L) and Puerto Rico (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Chile: 220V, 50Hz

Puerto Rico: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Chile 220V vs Puerto Rico 120V).
