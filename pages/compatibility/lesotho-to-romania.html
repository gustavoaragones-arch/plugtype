Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Romania; plug types used there differ from Lesotho.

Plug comparison

Lesotho plug types: M

Romania plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Lesotho (M) and Romania (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Lesotho: 230V, 50Hz

Romania: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
