Compatibility summary

Your Timor-Leste plug may fit in Maldives outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Timor-Leste plug types: C E F I

Maldives plug types: C D G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E F I and C D G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Timor-Leste: 230V, 50Hz

Maldives: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
