Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Qatar; plug types used there differ from Senegal.

Plug comparison

Senegal plug types: C E

Qatar plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Senegal (C E) and Qatar (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Senegal: 230V, 50Hz

Qatar: 240V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
