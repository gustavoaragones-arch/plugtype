Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in El Salvador; plug types used there differ from South Korea. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

South Korea plug types: C F

El Salvador plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between South Korea (C F) and El Salvador (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Korea: 220V, 60Hz

El Salvador: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (South Korea 220V vs El Salvador 120V).
