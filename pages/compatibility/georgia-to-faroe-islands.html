Compatibility summary

Your Georgia plug may fit in Faroe Islands outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Georgia plug types: C F

Faroe Islands plug types: C F K

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F K), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Georgia: 230V, 50Hz

Faroe Islands: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
