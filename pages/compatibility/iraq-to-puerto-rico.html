Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Puerto Rico; plug types used there differ from Iraq. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Iraq plug types: C G

Puerto Rico plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Iraq (C G) and Puerto Rico (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Iraq: 230V, 50Hz

Puerto Rico: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Iraq 230V vs Puerto Rico 120V).
