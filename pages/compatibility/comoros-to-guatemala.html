Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Guatemala; plug types used there differ from Comoros. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Comoros plug types: C E

Guatemala plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Comoros (C E) and Guatemala (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Comoros: 230V, 50Hz

Guatemala: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Comoros 230V vs Guatemala 120V).
