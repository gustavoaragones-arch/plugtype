Compatibility summary

Your Saint Kitts and Nevis plug may fit in Sierra Leone outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Saint Kitts and Nevis plug types: A B G

Sierra Leone plug types: G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B G and G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Saint Kitts and Nevis: 230V, 60Hz

Sierra Leone: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
