Can You Use a Singapore Plug in South Korea?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in South Korea; plug types used there differ from Singapore.

Plug comparison

Singapore plug types: G

South Korea plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Singapore (G) and South Korea (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Singapore: 230V, 50Hz

South Korea: 220V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
