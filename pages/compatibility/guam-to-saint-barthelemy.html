Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Barthélemy; plug types used there differ from Guam. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Guam plug types: A B

Saint Barthélemy plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Guam (A B) and Saint Barthélemy (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Guam: 110V, 60Hz

Saint Barthélemy: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Guam 110V vs Saint Barthélemy 230V).
