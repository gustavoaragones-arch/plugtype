Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Moldova; plug types used there differ from Mexico. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mexico plug types: A B

Moldova plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Mexico (A B) and Moldova (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Moldova: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mexico 127V vs Moldova 230V).
