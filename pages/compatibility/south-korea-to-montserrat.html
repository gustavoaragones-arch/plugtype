Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Montserrat; plug types used there differ from South Korea.

Plug comparison

South Korea plug types: C F

Montserrat plug types: A B G

There is no common plug type between South Korea (C F) and Montserrat (A B G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Korea: 220V, 60Hz

Montserrat: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
