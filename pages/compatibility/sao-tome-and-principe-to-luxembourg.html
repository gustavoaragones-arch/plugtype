Compatibility summary

Your São Tomé and Príncipe plug may fit in Luxembourg outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

São Tomé and Príncipe plug types: C F

Luxembourg plug types: C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Luxembourg: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
