Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Aruba; plug types used there differ from Armenia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Armenia plug types: C F

Aruba plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Armenia (C F) and Aruba (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Armenia: 230V, 50Hz

Aruba: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Armenia 230V vs Aruba 120V).
