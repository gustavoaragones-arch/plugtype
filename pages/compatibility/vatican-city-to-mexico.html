Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mexico; plug types used there differ from Vatican City. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Vatican City plug types: C F L

Mexico plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Vatican City (C F L) and Mexico (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Vatican City: 230V, 50Hz

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Vatican City 230V vs Mexico 127V).
