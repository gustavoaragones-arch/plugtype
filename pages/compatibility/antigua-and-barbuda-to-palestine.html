Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Palestine; plug types used there differ from Antigua and Barbuda.

Plug comparison

Antigua and Barbuda plug types: A B

Palestine plug types: C H

There is no common plug type between Antigua and Barbuda (A B) and Palestine (C H). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Antigua and Barbuda: 230V, 60Hz

Palestine: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
