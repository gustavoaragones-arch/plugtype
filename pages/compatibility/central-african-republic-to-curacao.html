Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Curaçao; plug types used there differ from Central African Republic. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Central African Republic plug types: C E

Curaçao plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Central African Republic (C E) and Curaçao (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Central African Republic: 230V, 50Hz

Curaçao: 127V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Central African Republic 230V vs Curaçao 127V).
