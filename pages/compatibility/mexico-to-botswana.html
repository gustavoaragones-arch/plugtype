Can You Use a Mexico Plug in Botswana?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Botswana; plug types used there differ from Mexico. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mexico plug types: A B

Botswana plug types: M

There is no common plug type between Mexico (A B) and Botswana (M). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

Botswana: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mexico 127V vs Botswana 230V).
