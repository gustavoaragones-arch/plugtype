Can You Use a United Kingdom Plug in Libya?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Libya; plug types used there differ from United Kingdom.

Plug comparison

United Kingdom plug types: G

Libya plug types: C F L

There is no common plug type between United Kingdom (G) and Libya (C F L). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

United Kingdom: 230V, 50Hz

Libya: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
