Can You Use a Mexico Plug in China?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your Mexico plug may fit in China outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Mexico plug types: A B

China plug types: A C I

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A C I), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Mexico: 127V, 60Hz

China: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Mexico 127V vs China 220V).
