Compatibility summary

Your Indonesia plug may fit in Thailand outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Indonesia plug types: C F

Thailand plug types: A B C O

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and A B C O), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Indonesia: 230V, 50Hz

Thailand: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
