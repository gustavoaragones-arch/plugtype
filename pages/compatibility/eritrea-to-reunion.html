Compatibility summary

Your Eritrea plug may fit in Réunion outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Eritrea plug types: C E L

Réunion plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C E L and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Eritrea: 230V, 50Hz

Réunion: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
