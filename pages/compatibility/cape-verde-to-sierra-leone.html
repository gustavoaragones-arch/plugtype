Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sierra Leone; plug types used there differ from Cape Verde.

Plug comparison

Cape Verde plug types: C F

Sierra Leone plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Cape Verde (C F) and Sierra Leone (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Cape Verde: 230V, 50Hz

Sierra Leone: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
