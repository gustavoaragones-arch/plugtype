Can You Use a Australia Plug in Germany?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Germany; plug types used there differ from Australia.

Plug comparison

Australia plug types: I

Germany plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Australia (I) and Germany (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Australia: 230V, 50Hz

Germany: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
