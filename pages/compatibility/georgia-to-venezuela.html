Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Venezuela; plug types used there differ from Georgia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Georgia plug types: C F

Venezuela plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Georgia (C F) and Venezuela (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Georgia: 230V, 50Hz

Venezuela: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Georgia 230V vs Venezuela 120V).
