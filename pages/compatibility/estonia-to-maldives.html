Compatibility summary

Your Estonia plug may fit in Maldives outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Estonia plug types: C F

Maldives plug types: C D G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C D G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Estonia: 230V, 50Hz

Maldives: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
