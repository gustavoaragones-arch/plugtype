Compatibility summary

Your South Sudan plug may fit in Senegal outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

South Sudan plug types: C D G

Senegal plug types: C E

Both countries share at least one plug type (C D G and C E), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

South Sudan: 230V, 50Hz

Senegal: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
