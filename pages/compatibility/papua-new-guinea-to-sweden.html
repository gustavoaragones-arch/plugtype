Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sweden; plug types used there differ from Papua New Guinea.

Plug comparison

Papua New Guinea plug types: I

Sweden plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Papua New Guinea (I) and Sweden (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Papua New Guinea: 240V, 50Hz

Sweden: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
