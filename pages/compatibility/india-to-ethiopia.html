Can You Use a India Plug in Ethiopia?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

Your India plug may fit in Ethiopia outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

India plug types: C D M

Ethiopia plug types: C F G

Both countries share at least one plug type (C D M and C F G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

India: 230V, 50Hz

Ethiopia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
