Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cayman Islands; plug types used there differ from Bosnia and Herzegovina. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Bosnia and Herzegovina plug types: C F

Cayman Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Bosnia and Herzegovina (C F) and Cayman Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Bosnia and Herzegovina: 230V, 50Hz

Cayman Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Bosnia and Herzegovina 230V vs Cayman Islands 120V).
