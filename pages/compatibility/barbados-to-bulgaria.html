Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bulgaria; plug types used there differ from Barbados. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Barbados plug types: A B

Bulgaria plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Barbados (A B) and Bulgaria (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Barbados: 115V, 50Hz

Bulgaria: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Barbados 115V vs Bulgaria 230V).
