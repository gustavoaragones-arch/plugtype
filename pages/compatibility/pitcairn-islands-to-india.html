Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in India; plug types used there differ from Pitcairn Islands.

Plug comparison

Pitcairn Islands plug types: I

India plug types: C D M

There is no common plug type between Pitcairn Islands (I) and India (C D M). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Pitcairn Islands: 230V, 50Hz

India: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
