Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Mauritania; plug types used there differ from Colombia. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Colombia plug types: A B

Mauritania plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Colombia (A B) and Mauritania (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Colombia: 110V, 60Hz

Mauritania: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Colombia 110V vs Mauritania 230V).
