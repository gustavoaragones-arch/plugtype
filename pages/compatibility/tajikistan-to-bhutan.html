Compatibility summary

Your Tajikistan plug may fit in Bhutan outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Tajikistan plug types: C F

Bhutan plug types: C D M

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C D M), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Tajikistan: 230V, 50Hz

Bhutan: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
