Can You Use a India Plug in Costa Rica?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Costa Rica; plug types used there differ from India. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

India plug types: C D M

Costa Rica plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between India (C D M) and Costa Rica (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

India: 230V, 50Hz

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (India 230V vs Costa Rica 120V).
