Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Iceland; plug types used there differ from Sri Lanka.

Plug comparison

Sri Lanka plug types: G

Iceland plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Sri Lanka (G) and Iceland (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Sri Lanka: 230V, 50Hz

Iceland: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
