Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Kitts and Nevis; plug types used there differ from Austria.

Plug comparison

Austria plug types: C F

Saint Kitts and Nevis plug types: A B G

There is no common plug type between Austria (C F) and Saint Kitts and Nevis (A B G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Austria: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Kitts and Nevis: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
