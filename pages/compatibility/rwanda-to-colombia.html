Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Colombia; plug types used there differ from Rwanda. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Rwanda plug types: C E F G

Colombia plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Rwanda (C E F G) and Colombia (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Rwanda: 230V, 50Hz

Colombia: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Rwanda 230V vs Colombia 110V).
