Can You Use a South Africa Plug in Saudi Arabia?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saudi Arabia; plug types used there differ from South Africa.

Plug comparison

South Africa plug types: C D M N

Saudi Arabia plug types: G

There is no common plug type between South Africa (C D M N) and Saudi Arabia (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

South Africa: 230V, 50Hz

Saudi Arabia: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
