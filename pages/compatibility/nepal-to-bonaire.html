Compatibility summary

Your Nepal plug may fit in Bonaire outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Nepal plug types: C D M

Bonaire plug types: A B C F

Both countries share at least one plug type (C D M and A B C F), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Nepal: 230V, 50Hz

Bonaire: 127V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Nepal 230V vs Bonaire 127V).
