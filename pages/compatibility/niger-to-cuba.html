Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cuba; plug types used there differ from Niger. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Niger plug types: C E

Cuba plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Niger (C E) and Cuba (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Niger: 230V, 50Hz

Cuba: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Niger 230V vs Cuba 120V).
