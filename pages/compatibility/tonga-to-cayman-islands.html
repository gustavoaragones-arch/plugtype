Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cayman Islands; plug types used there differ from Tonga. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Tonga plug types: I

Cayman Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Tonga (I) and Cayman Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Tonga: 240V, 50Hz

Cayman Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Tonga 240V vs Cayman Islands 120V).
