Compatibility summary

Your Saint Kitts and Nevis plug may fit in Turks and Caicos Islands outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Saint Kitts and Nevis plug types: A B G

Turks and Caicos Islands plug types: A B

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B G and A B), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Saint Kitts and Nevis: 230V, 60Hz

Turks and Caicos Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Saint Kitts and Nevis 230V vs Turks and Caicos Islands 120V).
