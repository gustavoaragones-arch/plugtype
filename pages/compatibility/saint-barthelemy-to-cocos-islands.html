Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cocos (Keeling) Islands; plug types used there differ from Saint Barthélemy.

Plug comparison

Saint Barthélemy plug types: C E

Cocos (Keeling) Islands plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Saint Barthélemy (C E) and Cocos (Keeling) Islands (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Barthélemy: 230V, 60Hz

Cocos (Keeling) Islands: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
