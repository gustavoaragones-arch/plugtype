Compatibility summary

Your Estonia plug may fit in Brazil outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Estonia plug types: C F

Brazil plug types: C N

Both countries share at least one plug type (C F and C N), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Estonia: 230V, 50Hz

Brazil: 127V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Estonia 230V vs Brazil 127V).
