Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Saint Vincent and the Grenadines; plug types used there differ from Burkina Faso.

Plug comparison

Burkina Faso plug types: C E

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines plug types: A B G

There is no common plug type between Burkina Faso (C E) and Saint Vincent and the Grenadines (A B G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Burkina Faso: 230V, 50Hz

Saint Vincent and the Grenadines: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
