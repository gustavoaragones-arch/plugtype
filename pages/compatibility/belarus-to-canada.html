Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Canada; plug types used there differ from Belarus. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Belarus plug types: C F

Canada plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Belarus (C F) and Canada (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Belarus: 230V, 50Hz

Canada: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Belarus 230V vs Canada 120V).
