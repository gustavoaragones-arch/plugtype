Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Haiti; plug types used there differ from Uruguay. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Uruguay plug types: C F L

Haiti plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Uruguay (C F L) and Haiti (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Uruguay: 230V, 50Hz

Haiti: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Uruguay 230V vs Haiti 110V).
