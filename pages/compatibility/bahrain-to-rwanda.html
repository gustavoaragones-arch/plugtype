Compatibility summary

Your Bahrain plug may fit in Rwanda outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Bahrain plug types: G

Rwanda plug types: C E F G

Both countries share at least one plug type (G and C E F G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Bahrain: 230V, 50Hz

Rwanda: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
