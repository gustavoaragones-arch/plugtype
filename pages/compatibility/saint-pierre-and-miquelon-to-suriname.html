Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Suriname; plug types used there differ from Saint Pierre and Miquelon. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon plug types: C E

Suriname plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Saint Pierre and Miquelon (C E) and Suriname (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Saint Pierre and Miquelon: 230V, 50Hz

Suriname: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Saint Pierre and Miquelon 230V vs Suriname 110V).
