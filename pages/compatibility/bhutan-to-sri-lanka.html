Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sri Lanka; plug types used there differ from Bhutan.

Plug comparison

Bhutan plug types: C D M

Sri Lanka plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Bhutan (C D M) and Sri Lanka (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Bhutan: 230V, 50Hz

Sri Lanka: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
