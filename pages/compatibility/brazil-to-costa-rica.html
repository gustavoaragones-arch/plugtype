Can You Use a Brazil Plug in Costa Rica?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Costa Rica; plug types used there differ from Brazil.

Plug comparison

Brazil plug types: C N

Costa Rica plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Brazil (C N) and Costa Rica (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Brazil: 127V, 60Hz

Costa Rica: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
