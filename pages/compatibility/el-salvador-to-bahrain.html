Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Bahrain; plug types used there differ from El Salvador. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

El Salvador plug types: A B

Bahrain plug types: G

There is no common plug type between El Salvador (A B) and Bahrain (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

El Salvador: 120V, 60Hz

Bahrain: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (El Salvador 120V vs Bahrain 230V).
