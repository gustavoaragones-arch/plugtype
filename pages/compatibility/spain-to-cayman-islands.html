Can You Use a Spain Plug in Cayman Islands?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Cayman Islands; plug types used there differ from Spain. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Spain plug types: C F

Cayman Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Spain (C F) and Cayman Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Spain: 230V, 50Hz

Cayman Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Spain 230V vs Cayman Islands 120V).
