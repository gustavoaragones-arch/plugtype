Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Angola; plug types used there differ from Sierra Leone.

Plug comparison

Sierra Leone plug types: G

Angola plug types: C F

There is no common plug type between Sierra Leone (G) and Angola (C F). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Sierra Leone: 230V, 50Hz

Angola: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
