Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Sint Maarten; plug types used there differ from Chad. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Chad plug types: C E F

Sint Maarten plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Chad (C E F) and Sint Maarten (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Chad: 230V, 50Hz

Sint Maarten: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Chad 230V vs Sint Maarten 110V).
