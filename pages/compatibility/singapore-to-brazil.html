Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Brazil; plug types used there differ from Singapore. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Singapore plug types: G

Brazil plug types: C N

There is no common plug type between Singapore (G) and Brazil (C N). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Singapore: 230V, 50Hz

Brazil: 127V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Singapore 230V vs Brazil 127V).
