Can You Use a Italy Plug in Malta?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Malta; plug types used there differ from Italy.

Plug comparison

Italy plug types: C F L

Malta plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Italy (C F L) and Malta (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Italy: 230V, 50Hz

Malta: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
