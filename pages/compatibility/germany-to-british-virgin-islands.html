Can You Use a Germany Plug in British Virgin Islands?

← Compatibility tool

Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in British Virgin Islands; plug types used there differ from Germany. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Germany plug types: C F

British Virgin Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Germany (C F) and British Virgin Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Germany: 230V, 50Hz

British Virgin Islands: 110V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Germany 230V vs British Virgin Islands 110V).
