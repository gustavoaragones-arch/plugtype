Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Isle of Man; plug types used there differ from Ukraine.

Plug comparison

Ukraine plug types: C F

Isle of Man plug types: G

There is no common plug type between Ukraine (C F) and Isle of Man (G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Ukraine: 230V, 50Hz

Isle of Man: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
