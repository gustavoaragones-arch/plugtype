Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in New Caledonia; plug types used there differ from Sint Maarten. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Sint Maarten plug types: A B

New Caledonia plug types: C E

There is no common plug type between Sint Maarten (A B) and New Caledonia (C E). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Sint Maarten: 110V, 60Hz

New Caledonia: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Sint Maarten 110V vs New Caledonia 230V).
