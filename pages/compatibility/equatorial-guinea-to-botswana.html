Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Botswana; plug types used there differ from Equatorial Guinea.

Plug comparison

Equatorial Guinea plug types: C E

Botswana plug types: M

There is no common plug type between Equatorial Guinea (C E) and Botswana (M). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Equatorial Guinea: 230V, 50Hz

Botswana: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
