Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Tokelau; plug types used there differ from Bangladesh.

Plug comparison

Bangladesh plug types: A C D M G

Tokelau plug types: I

There is no common plug type between Bangladesh (A C D M G) and Tokelau (I). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Bangladesh: 230V, 50Hz

Tokelau: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
