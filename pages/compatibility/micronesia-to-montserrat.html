Compatibility summary

Your Micronesia plug may fit in Montserrat outlets, as both use at least one common plug type. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Micronesia plug types: A B

Montserrat plug types: A B G

Both countries share at least one plug type (A B and A B G), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Micronesia: 120V, 60Hz

Montserrat: 230V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Micronesia 120V vs Montserrat 230V).
