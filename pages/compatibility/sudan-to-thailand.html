Compatibility summary

Your Sudan plug may fit in Thailand outlets, as both use at least one common plug type.

Plug comparison

Sudan plug types: C D G

Thailand plug types: A B C O

Both countries share at least one plug type (C D G and A B C O), so some plugs may work without an adapter. Check your appliance.

Voltage comparison

Sudan: 230V, 50Hz

Thailand: 220V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
