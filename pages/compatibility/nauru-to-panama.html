Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Panama; plug types used there differ from Nauru. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Nauru plug types: I

Panama plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Nauru (I) and Panama (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Nauru: 240V, 50Hz

Panama: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Nauru 240V vs Panama 120V).
