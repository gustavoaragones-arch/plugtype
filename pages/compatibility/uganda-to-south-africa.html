Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in South Africa; plug types used there differ from Uganda.

Plug comparison

Uganda plug types: G

South Africa plug types: C D M N

There is no common plug type between Uganda (G) and South Africa (C D M N). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Uganda: 240V, 50Hz

South Africa: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage difference is within a range many devices tolerate; check your appliance label.
