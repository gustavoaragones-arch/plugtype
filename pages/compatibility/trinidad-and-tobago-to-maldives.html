Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Maldives; plug types used there differ from Trinidad and Tobago. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Trinidad and Tobago plug types: A B

Maldives plug types: C D G

There is no common plug type between Trinidad and Tobago (A B) and Maldives (C D G). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Trinidad and Tobago: 115V, 60Hz

Maldives: 230V, 50Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Trinidad and Tobago 115V vs Maldives 230V).
