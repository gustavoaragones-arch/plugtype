Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Marshall Islands; plug types used there differ from Syria. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Syria plug types: C E F L

Marshall Islands plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Syria (C E F L) and Marshall Islands (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Syria: 230V, 50Hz

Marshall Islands: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Syria 230V vs Marshall Islands 120V).
