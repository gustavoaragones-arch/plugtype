Compatibility summary

You will need a travel adapter in Palau; plug types used there differ from Solomon Islands. Voltage differs by more than 20% — a voltage converter may be needed for some appliances.

Plug comparison

Solomon Islands plug types: I

Palau plug types: A B

There is no common plug type between Solomon Islands (I) and Palau (A B). You need a travel adapter.

Voltage comparison

Solomon Islands: 230V, 50Hz

Palau: 120V, 60Hz

Voltage converter recommended (Solomon Islands 230V vs Palau 120V).
